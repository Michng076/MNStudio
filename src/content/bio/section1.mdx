Michael Nguyen is a versatile musician, esteemed tenor, pianist, and dedicated private lessons teacher. Holding a **Bachelor of Music in Vocal Performance and Choral Music Education** from the prestigious University of North Texas, he received tutelage from the renowned soprano, Dr. Carol Wilson, and performed under the baton of Dr. Allen Hightower in the UNT A Cappella Choir and Vox Aquilae early music ensemble.
