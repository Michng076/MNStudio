  During his time at UNT, Michael's exceptional talent and hard work garnered numerous accolades. He secured **first place** in the 2021 Texoma National Association of Teachers of Singing Competition, as well as **first place** in the 2021 and 2019 DFW NATS competitions. Additionally, he achieved **second place** in the 2020 Texoma NATS and reached the finals in the 2020 DFW NATS. Notably, Michael was selected to sing the National Anthem at both the 2022 UNT Graduation Ceremony and the 2022 Plano Balloon Festival, showcasing his captivating vocal prowess.
