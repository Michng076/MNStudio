   As a voice teacher, Michael possesses a comprehensive skill set to expertly guide students through audition processes and solo contests. Drawing from his own experience as a **two-time Texas All-Stater**, where he excelled as **1st chair Tenor 2**, he has an innate understanding of the demands and expectations of such events. His achievements extend beyond the realm of vocal competitions, as he made his **vocal debut with the Baylor Symphony Orchestra** after triumphing as the **Senior Grand Prize Division winner at the 2018 Dallas Symphonic Festival.** Moreover, he has been recognized for his outstanding performances, including a first-place finish in the 2018 Dallas Music Teachers Association Vocal Solo Performance Contest, and he went on to receive **first-place at the state level (TMTA).**
